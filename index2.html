Latitude vs. Temperature (Farenheit)

This plot demonstrates a trend in randomly selected cities by latitudinal location, plotted on the x-axis, vs maximum temperature of that city in degrees Farenheit on the y-axis. There appears to be a generally negative trend; as city Latitude increases, temperature decreases. More accurately, as latitude gets closer to the equator, temeratures increase. This is a reasonable conclusion to draw, and an expected prediction. The further a city is from the Earth's equator, the more likley that city will be colder.
